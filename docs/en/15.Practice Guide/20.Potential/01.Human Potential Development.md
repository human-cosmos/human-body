## Level 1: Physiology & Structure

### Self-Healing & Regeneration

**Phenomenon:** Under deep relaxation, body secretes大量 stem cells, achieving unusual self-healing.

**Scientific explanation:**

- Deep relaxation activates parasympathetic nervous system
- Cortisol level降低
- Immune system efficiency提升
- Stem cell activity增强

**Examples:**

Some meditators report "unexplainable" body improvements—chronic pain消失, wound healing加速, diseases self-healing. This isn't mystical power, it's inherent capability manifesting.

**Research evidence:**

- Dr. Herbert Benson's relaxation response research at Harvard
- Studies showing meditation increases immune function markers
- Cases of spontaneous healing under deep meditative states

### Sensory Amplification

**Phenomenon:** Ability to capture extremely weak signals enhances.

**Forms:**

- **Night vision enhancement** — Seeing better in darkness
- **Auditory sensitivity提升** — Filtering noise, hearing distant sounds
- **Olfactory enhancement** — Sensing subtle scents
- **Touch sensitivity** — Sensing微 vibrations

**Principle:**

Brain's filter widens, allowing more sensory information into awareness. Also, when fascia relaxes, whole body becomes a larger "sensor."

### Environmental Adaptation

**Phenomenon:** Consciousness接管 autonomic nervous system, achieving extreme environment adaptation.

**Case: Wim Hof (The Iceman)**

Dutchman Wim Hof, through specific breathing and meditation techniques, achieved:
- Over 2 hours in ice water
- -20°C environment wearing only shorts
- Conscious immune system control

Research: Radboud University studies验证 his method's effects on immune system.

**Principle:**

Meditation training enables awareness to influence autonomic nervous system, thereby controlling temperature, immune response and other "automatic" functions.

**Development path:**

1. Deep breathing training (Wim Hof method)
2. Consciousness focusing on specific physiological processes
3. Building consciousness-autonomic nerve connection
4. Gradually extending control范围

## Development Misconceptions

### Pursuing Mystical Experiences

Potential development过程 may accompany various experiences—light, sound, vibration, OBE, etc. But these are byproducts, not goals.

Pursuing experiences反而 blocks true progress.

### Skipping Levels

Pursuing high-level abilities without foundation, like building skyscraper without foundation—problems必然 arise.

Common issues:
- Energy flow不畅 causing physical不适
- Thought过度 active causing anxiety
- Mental experiences过度 causing reality disconnection

### Function Attachment

After obtaining某 capability, attaching to it反而 consumes energy, causing capability退化.

Correct attitude: Capabilities naturally manifest, not for炫耀. Use when present, don't pursue when absent.

## Safety Principles

### Four Safety Principles

1. **Don't use thought to forcefully guide** — All energy guidance is natural, not pushed
2. **Don't pursue mystical experiences** — Experiences come—accept; don't come—don't expect
3. **Step-by-step progression** — Start from foundation, don't skip
4. **Maintain reality connection** — Don't脱离 daily life, practice serves life

### Danger Signals

Immediately stop and seek help:
- Persistent dizziness, headache
- Reality sense loss
- Strong fear sensation
- Energy running异常 (like持续 rushing to head)
- Severe sleep disruption
